DMCA / Copyright Policy

This policy explains how to report alleged copyright infringement involving JustAidyn content, user submissions, or linked materials.

Last updated: April 25, 2026
ComplaintCounter-noticeRepeat infringers

Copyright Complaint

Send copyright complaints to aidyn.daulet@gmail.com. Include:

  • Your name, organization if any, email, and contact information.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work you claim is infringed.
  • The exact URL or location of the allegedly infringing material.
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorized.
  • A statement that the information is accurate and that you are the owner or authorized agent.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Counter-Notice

If your material was removed and you believe this was a mistake, contact us with your identity, the removed material, your good-faith explanation, and your consent to the relevant legal process where required.

Repeat Infringers

We may restrict or terminate accounts or access for repeated copyright infringement or abuse of the complaint process.
