Motivational letter and agreement

Two versions of the same course agreement: for ages 16-17 and for 18+.

How enrollment works

  1. First, print this document.
  2. Fill all fields except payment points and send it to aidyn.daulet@gmail.com. All communication continues in the same email thread.
  3. The email subject must be: "Group name, course name". If you decide to change groups in the future, the motivational letter must be sent again as a new message.
  4. After instructor approval, the client pays and fills the same document in payment fields. Enrollment is considered complete only after that. Payment link/QR is provided after approval.

Important before enrollment

This is a live online course: classes happen in real time, with practice and active participation. Between meetings, you will need to test, build, and gradually integrate tools into your work, so both time and a calm state matter.

From experience, the course works best for people who already have stable income, roughly from 350,000 KZT per month and above.

Not because the course is for someone special, but because then learning goes without extra pressure: no need to rush to recover payment, sacrifice sleep, or try to do everything at the last minute.

When both time and financial freedom are limited at the same time, people start learning in bursts: they skip stages, worry, get tired, and the benefit is lower than it could be.

That is why participation is split into different formats by depth of involvement, so each person can choose a pace they can realistically complete to result.

If this is a period of overload now, or it is better to stabilize core matters first, that is normal. You can return to the course later and complete it much more calmly and productively.

It is important to me that learning helps in life, and does not add extra tension.
