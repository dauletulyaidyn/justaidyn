Auto-Renewal and Billing

If a plan is shown as recurring, it renews automatically at the end of each billing period until cancelled. The checkout must disclose the price, billing period, renewal behavior, and cancellation method before payment.

If a price changes, we will provide notice where required by law. Continued use after the effective change may require acceptance of the new price or terms.

Cancellation Effects

Cancellation stops future renewal charges. It does not refund the current paid period. Access continues until the paid period expires unless your account is terminated for misuse.
