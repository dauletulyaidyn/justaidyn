Auto-Renewal and Billing

If a plan is shown as recurring, it renews automatically at the end of each billing period until cancelled. The checkout must disclose the price, billing period, renewal behavior, and cancellation method before payment.

If a price changes, we will provide notice where required by law. Continued use after the effective change may require acceptance of the new price or terms.

Cancellation Effects

Cancellation stops future renewal charges. It does not refund the current paid period. Access continues until the paid period expires unless your account is terminated for misuse.

Account Deletion and Active Subscriptions

If you delete your JustAidyn account while you have an active paid subscription, the following applies:

  • Subscription cancellation: Your subscription is cancelled automatically when you delete your account. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain access until that date.
  • No refund: Deleting your account does not entitle you to a refund for the remaining days of the current billing period. The no-refund policy described in the Cancellation section applies.
  • Confirmation required: Before your account is deleted, you will be shown a clear warning that your subscription will be cancelled and no refund will be issued. You must confirm before deletion proceeds.
  • Re-registration: If you create a new account with the same Google account after deletion, any previously cancelled subscription is not reinstated. You must subscribe again to regain access to paid features.
  • Data removal: Your profile data, sessions, and subscription linkage are removed from our systems on deletion. Paddle retains its own billing records in accordance with its privacy policy and applicable law.

If you wish to stop charges without losing your account, cancel your subscription from the app page instead of deleting your account. Cancellation stops future renewal charges while keeping your account active.
