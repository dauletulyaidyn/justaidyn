Data We May Collect

  • Account data such as name, email, login provider, and authentication identifiers.
  • Subscription and access data such as plan, product, status, start date, cancellation date, and entitlement history.
  • Support data such as messages, attachments, email, WhatsApp, or Telegram contact details you provide.
  • Technical data such as IP-derived country if a payment or security provider supplies it, device data, logs, cookie choices, and analytics events.

How We Use Data

We use data to provide accounts, subscriptions, downloads, content access, applications, games, support, security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and site improvement.

We do not sell personal data. Optional analytics or marketing cookies are used only after consent where required.

Payments

Payment details are processed by external payment providers. JustAidyn should receive only payment status, transaction identifiers, subscription status, and limited billing metadata needed to provide access, accounting, support, and legal compliance.

Cookies

Necessary cookies and local storage support region selection, cookie consent, security, and site operation. Analytics, marketing, and preference cookies are optional where consent is required.

Children's Privacy

JustAidyn paid subscriptions are not directed to children under 13. If a service is used by minors, a parent, legal guardian, school, or authorized adult should supervise access where required by law.

If you believe a child provided personal data without appropriate consent, contact us and we will review deletion or account restriction requests.

Where GDPR applies, you may have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and withdrawal of consent. Legal bases may include contract, consent, legal obligation, and legitimate interests.
