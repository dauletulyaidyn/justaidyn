No-Refund Policy for Paid Digital Access

All purchases of paid digital subscriptions are final once access begins. This is because you receive immediate access to digital content, digital services, applications, or games.

No refunds or partial refunds are provided for an active paid period after access starts, except where mandatory law requires otherwise or where JustAidyn decides to make an exception in writing.

Cancellation

You may cancel a paid subscription to stop future renewals. Cancellation does not refund the current paid period. Access normally continues until the end of the already paid billing period.

If the payment provider offers subscription management, cancellation may need to be completed through that provider or through your JustAidyn account once available.

Covered Products

  • NoFaceThinker paid content subscription.
  • Paid subscription for an individual application.
  • Paid all-games subscription.
  • Any future paid digital content subscription that references this policy.

SkillsAndMindsHub free access does not involve paid refunds unless a future paid tier is added.

Regional Terms

The selected regional notice below explains how the no-refund policy is applied.

Kazakhstan

For Kazakhstan users, paid subscriptions are digital content or digital service access. After access begins, the paid period is non-refundable unless mandatory Kazakhstan law requires otherwise.

EU / EEA

If EU withdrawal rights apply, you may lose the 14-day withdrawal right when you expressly consent to immediate digital access and acknowledge that this right is lost once performance begins. The checkout must require this acknowledgement before paid immediate access.

United Kingdom

If UK cancellation rights apply, immediate supply of digital content may remove cancellation rights after you expressly consent and acknowledge the effect of immediate access.

United States / California

For automatically renewing subscriptions, cancellation stops future billing. California users must receive clear renewal terms and an easy cancellation method before renewal charges continue.

Other Regions

General no-refund terms apply unless mandatory local law gives you non-waivable refund or cancellation rights.
